Welcome to Seattle Pet Adoption

Looking to add a furry friend to your life? Seattle is home to wonderful shelters and rescues that need loving forever homes.

Top Adoption Organizations

  • Seattle Animal Shelter (City)
  • Seattle Humane
  • Seattle Area Feline Rescue (SAFe)
  • Underdog Pet Rescue (North Seattle)

Why Adopt?

  • Saves lives and reduces demand for puppy mills
  • Adoption fees usually include spay/neuter, vaccines, microchip
  • Helps free up shelter space for others
